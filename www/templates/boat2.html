Your Paddles

The first step in Building Your Boat is figuring out how you are powering it.
You can either power it with your own mucles (by Rowing Your Boat) or you can use an Engine.
The majority of us have to Actively Row Our Boats (actively go to work to earn a living), but the wealthy know how to use Engines (they have a system to generate money for them) so they don't have to actively go to work.
In this calculator, enter your amounts you make per month from your jobs or profession. This total is your Boats' Paddle Power.
