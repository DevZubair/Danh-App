Step: Active Incomes

"What are Quiet Incomes?" A "quiet income" is a term I coined myself to describe the types of incomes I stumbled upon that consistently and quietly appeared in my bank account month after month without me having to go out there and do physical work for.

You may have heard others refer to this as PASSIVE INCOME. Passive incomes are very powerful incomes, and it is what this whole program is all about.

But before you can appreciate passive incomes, it's better to go over what passive incomes are not. To do this, let's look at our typical incomes.

Our Typical Income From Work

Most of us earn our living through what we call ACTIVE INCOMES. When you have to wake up each morning, drive yourself to work and spend 8-12 hours a day doing something to earn a living, you are making money via an active income. That is because you have to ACTIVELY participate with your TIME and ENERGY in order to earn that income.

Ninety percent of us earn a living with active incomes because that is how society was built. We achieve wonderful things at work as every job is focused around building better products to improve our lives or providing much needed services to our friends and neighbors.

Nothing can replace this value. But at the same time, these jobs are both LABOR INTENSIVE and TIME CONSUMING.

When we only earn income from Active Incomes, we are faced with limitations we can't control because:

  • We only have so much TIME to work!
  • We only have so much ENERGY to work!
And if you try to earn lots of money through an ACTIVE INCOME, what will be the end result?
  • You Will Run Out of Time to Work (did you forget your friends, family, or your health?)
  • You Will Run Out of Energy to Work (can you still run around and play with the kids or hike that mountain you've always wanted to?)

So is it any surprise to see so many of us striving for success with our careers only to end up being TOO TIRED and having NO TIME LEFT to enjoy Life?

That's Where I Was When I Decided to Change

Somewhere in the middle of life, I found myself doing exactly what I loved...practicing medicine. However, working 60-80 hours a week and following the steps society have set up for us have left me out of touch with all my old friends. On top of that, I barely have any time left to spend with my aging parents, my new wife, and the new kids that I finally was supposed to have time for.

Aside from the joys of practicing medicine, this wasn't how I envisioned my life would be...

Lesson on Active Incomes

ACTIVE INCOMES require you to continuously put in TIME and ENERGY to generate money.
